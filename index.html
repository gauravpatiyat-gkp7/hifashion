Questions, answered

What sizes does Hi Fashion carry?

Every style is stocked from XS to 4X, with plus sizes on the rack in store. Alterations are free, so the fit is finished to you.

Where is Hi Fashion located?

We're at 10 Pillappa, Rajanna Lane, Nagarthpet Cross, Nagarathpete, Bengaluru 560002. Open Mon–Sat 11 AM–8 PM and Sunday 11 AM–2 PM.

Can I shop without visiting the store?

Yes. Shortlist online and we'll hold your picks in the trial room for 48 hours, or shop over a WhatsApp video call and we'll walk you through the rack live.

What occasions do you stock for?

Haldi, Sangeet, Mehendi, Cocktail, Wedding and Festive — kurta sets, co-ords, shararas, drapes, lehengas and Indo-Western, from workday to reception.
